Frequently Asked Questions and Support Resources

General Questions

What services does Team4Stack offer?

Team4Stack offers a comprehensive range of full-stack development services including:

  • Web Development (React, Next.js, MERN Stack)
  • Backend Development (Node.js, Express, MongoDB, PostgreSQL)
  • Mobile App Development (React Native)
  • UI/UX Design
  • API Integrations
  • Cloud Deployment & DevOps
How long does a typical project take to complete?

Project timelines vary depending on complexity and scope. Here are our general timeframes:

  • Simple Website: 2-4 weeks
  • Complex Web Application: 2-6 months
  • Mobile App: 3-8 months
  • E-commerce Platform: 4-10 months

We provide detailed project timelines during our initial consultation based on your specific requirements.

Do you offer ongoing maintenance and support?

Yes, we offer comprehensive maintenance and support packages for all our projects. Our support options include:

  • 24/7 monitoring and uptime guarantees
  • Regular security updates and patches
  • Performance optimization
  • Bug fixes and troubleshooting
  • Feature enhancements and updates

Contact us to discuss the best support package for your project.

Development Process

What is your development process?

Our development process follows these key stages:

  1. Discovery: We discuss your requirements, goals, and target audience.
  2. Planning: We create a detailed project plan, timeline, and technical specifications.
  3. Design: Our UI/UX team creates wireframes and design mockups for your approval.
  4. Development: We build your project using agile methodologies with regular updates.
  5. Testing: Comprehensive testing across devices and browsers.
  6. Deployment: We launch your project and provide training if needed.
  7. Maintenance: Ongoing support and updates as required.
How do you handle project communication and updates?

We believe in transparent and frequent communication throughout the project lifecycle:

  • Weekly progress reports and status meetings
  • Dedicated project management dashboard for real-time updates
  • Direct access to your project manager and lead developer
  • Regular milestone reviews and feedback sessions
  • Slack or email communication for quick questions

We use project management tools like Jira, Trello, or Asana to keep everything organized and visible.

What technologies do you specialize in?

We specialize in modern, industry-standard technologies:

Frontend:

  • React.js, Next.js, Vue.js
  • HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, TypeScript
  • Responsive design frameworks (Bootstrap, Tailwind CSS)

Backend:

  • Node.js with Express
  • Python with Django/Flask
  • Database design (MongoDB, PostgreSQL, MySQL)

Mobile:

  • React Native
  • Progressive Web Apps (PWA)

DevOps:

  • AWS, Azure, Google Cloud Platform
  • Docker, Kubernetes
  • CI/CD pipelines

Billing and Contracts

How do you handle billing and payments?

We offer flexible billing options to suit your needs:

  • Fixed Price: For well-defined projects with clear requirements
  • Time and Materials: For ongoing development or projects with evolving requirements
  • Retainer: Monthly packages for continuous development work

Payment terms typically include:

  • Initial deposit (usually 30-50%) upon contract signing
  • Progress payments at project milestones
  • Final payment upon project completion and approval

We accept payments via bank transfer, credit card, and PayPal.

What is your refund policy?

Our refund policy is as follows:

  • Deposits are non-refundable once work has commenced
  • Refunds may be considered within the first 14 days for fixed-price projects if no work has been completed
  • No refunds for work already delivered and accepted
  • Refunds for time and materials projects are considered on a case-by-case basis

We strive to ensure complete satisfaction with our work, and we work closely with clients throughout the process to avoid any issues.

Technical Support

How do I report a bug or technical issue?

If you encounter a bug or technical issue with your project, please report it through one of these channels:

  • Email our support team at support@team4stack.com
  • Submit a ticket through our client portal
  • Contact your project manager directly

When reporting an issue, please include:

  • Detailed description of the problem
  • Steps to reproduce the issue
  • Screenshots if applicable
  • Your environment details (browser, device, etc.)

Response times vary by support level but are typically within 24 hours for standard issues.

Do you provide training for our team?

Yes, we offer comprehensive training for your team to ensure smooth adoption and ongoing management of your project:

  • Admin panel training for content management
  • User role and permission setup
  • Basic troubleshooting and maintenance procedures
  • Documentation and best practices
  • Follow-up sessions as needed

Training can be conducted in-person, via video conference, or through detailed documentation, depending on your preference and project requirements.
